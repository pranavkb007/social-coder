"Best Poet Award"

  1. Home
      2. "Best Poet Award"

Award Details

  • Category: State Level
  • Honoured by: Founder, Pune Poets
  • Award date: 12 Jul 2022
  • Venue: Wagholi, Pune

At the state-level intercollegiate poetry competition held in Pune, over 50 poets from various districts of Maharashtra presented their poems. In this prestigious competition, poet Suraj Sunil Shirale won second place. Suraj Shirale presented a poem on the Battle of Pratapgad led by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, which was well received by both the judges and the audience. The organizers honored him with a certificate and a cash prize of 1000 rupees, along with much appreciation for his poetry.
