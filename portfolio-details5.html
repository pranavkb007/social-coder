Award Details

  • Category: National Level
  • Honoured by: Chairman, IRC
  • Award date: 23 April 2023
  • Venue: Kurukshetra, Harayana

Mamasaheb Mohol College from Maharashtra excelled at the National Youth Red Cross Camp in Kurukshetra, Haryana, held from March 14 to 21. Representing Maharashtra, they competed against sixteen states, winning first place in group dance and third in drama, and were honored as the best team overall. Mr. Suraj Sunil Shirale engaged with the youth on national unity, wrote about national integration, and actively participated in various activities, earning praise from attendees.
